About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Wright State University, advised by Prof. Fathi Amsaad. My research focuses on quantum-classical hybrid optimization algorithms for large-scale biological datasets, with a particular interest in distributed machine learning and high-dimensional data processing.

Research Interests

  • Quantum Machine Learning
  • High-Dimensional Clustering
  • Distributed Optimization
  • Computational Genomics
